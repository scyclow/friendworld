When was the last time you were truely happy?

Every day, you wake up and feel the same way: terrible. You dread getting out of bed, and you hate what your life has become. You've been running around the same hamster wheel for years, and you don't know how to get off. One day you're a bright eyed-child with dreams and fantasies -- you're going to grow up and change the world, and live life to the fullest. But then you blink, and it's all behind you. All that time wasted. All that potential gone. And there's nothing you can do about it. You've lost control of your own brain as it does whatever it can to stay above water. At the slightest hint of anxiety, you self-medicate by pulling out your phone to cycle around the same few apps or websites. It doesn't make anything better, but it helps you forget what you're feeling. It all just feels so empty. And you're trapped. There's no way out.

But what if I told you that you don't have to feel the way you do right now?

  • The feeling of emptiness you get as you do things you once loved? Gone.
  • That lethargy and sense of hopelessness that seems to hang over you like a cloud throughout the day? Gone.
  • The feeling of dread and anger that won't leave the pit of your stomach no matter how hard you try? Gone.
  • The feeling that something, or someone, is watching you, recording all of your activities, and using them against you to further their own agenda? Gone.

All these things can be gone from your life as long as you answer one simple question: Do I want to feel this way?
